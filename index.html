About Me

I was first introduced to software engineering and coding by what’s called modding, the modification of video game files in a way that makes an entirely different games. The ability to bring life to an idea brought me a sense of elation and pride. Although my initial fascination with modding has waned, my passion for coding has only grown stronger. What was once a love of modding has turned to a love of web design.

I graduated from the University of Texas with an honors degree in Government in 2012. My education instilled a strong work ethic and a drive for perfection in all that I do. Currently, I am sharpening my skills as a full-stack web developer through the University of Texas’ Coding Bootcamp. For all inquires, please email me at brianstewart1912@gmail.com
